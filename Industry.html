IT

Values | Industry

The Information Technology (IT) industry describes a large field that involves the development, implementation, and maintenance of computer systems, networks, and databases. IT is a vitial part of almost every other industry, business, or organization no matter the size.

Careers

IT has a wide variety of careers, all ranging on a employee's skill set, interests, and responsiblites. One job that interests me is a Network Engineer. A network engineer is responsible for the planning, designing, and implementation of many different kinds of networks. Network engineers are responsible for maintaining and then troubleshooting networks & analyzing network traffic.

Ethics

One current ethical issue in the IT industry is how the collection of user data is current being handled. As of right now, user data is being collected on mass for a variety of different purposes, ranging from development to security anaylsis. But often times users are unware of how much data is being collected and how this data is often insecure. I believe that the industry needs to shift to be more honest in what data is collected and how it is used.

Security

IT plays a major role in ensuring the security of networks and in the education of users. A current security issue is the rising number of AI-releated cyber attacks. With the assistance of AI tools low-level hackers or "Script Kiddies" are now able to launch increasinly more susificated and more frequent attacks. IT professionals must be well-informed on on common secuirty practices such as, removing the default password on IoT devices, not clicking on suspicious links or emails, and securing all networks with ACLS and firewalls.
