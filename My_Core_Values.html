Integrity

1. I believe that you should do what is right, no matter the consequences.
2. I think that for the world to function properly everyone should be honest and accountable.
3. During school my teachers pushed me to be responsible for my actions.
4. I believe that it is important to be honest throughout my personal and professional life.

Adaptibility

1. I believe that it is important to be able to adjust to new situations.
2. Without adaptability, we cannot improve ourselves or the world.
3. My family taught that being prepared for changes was key to success.
4. I try to embrace change and adapt to new situations.

Communication

1. I believe that you should treat everyone with respect.
2. I try to listen to others and understand their perspectives.
3. I have an open mind and I'm willing to hear everyone's opinion.
4. I try to leave a branch open for different ideas.

Patience

1. I believe that patience helps us make better decisions.
2. I try to avoid panicking and instead remain calm when faced with challenges.
3. I try to take my time to examine all my choices before making a decision.
4. I believe that being patient is vital for making and keeoing relationships.
